Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Resume SuperHero ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information from Account Creation

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (first and last)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Job title and career stage (optional)
  • Industry and location (optional)

Resume Data

Your resume content (text, formatting, work history, education, and skills) is collected when you upload or input it. Resume data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. You maintain full control and ownership of your resume data at all times.

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your interactions with our Service:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Features used and frequency of use
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Referral source

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, maintain your session, and understand user behavior. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality of the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve Resume SuperHero and deliver the features and functionality you request
  • • Account Management: To manage your account, process payments, and send transactional emails
  • • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and send service-related announcements
  • • Marketing: To send promotional emails about new features, updates, and special offers (you can opt-out anytime)
  • • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns, improve our Service, and develop new features
  • • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests
  • • Security: To prevent fraud, abuse, and protect the security of our Service and users

3. Data Storage and Security

Resume Data Storage

Your resume data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. We do not transmit, store, or process your resume content on our servers. This means your resume remains on your device and under your control. When you clear your browser cache or delete localStorage data, your saved resumes will be removed.

Account Information Security

Your account information (email, name, password) is stored securely on encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Password hashing using bcrypt or similar algorithms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to databases and systems
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving your personal information, we will notify you without undue delay via email or through a prominent notice on our website, and we will cooperate with law enforcement as required.

Note: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Payment Processing (Stripe)

Resume SuperHero uses Stripe to process subscription payments. When you subscribe to our service ($9.99/month), your payment information is handled directly by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.

We do not store your credit card information on our servers. For details on how Stripe handles your payment data, please review their Privacy Policy.

Analytics and Third-Party Tools

We may use third-party analytics tools to understand how users interact with our Service. These tools may collect anonymized usage data. We do not share personally identifiable information with these services without your consent.

Links to Third-Party Websites

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before providing your information.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.

Right to Data Portability

You can request that we provide your personal information in a portable format.

Right to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of marketing communications and non-essential cookies at any time.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at partners@theaisuperheroes.com with details of your request.

6. Data Retention

Account Information

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Resume Data

Since your resume is stored locally in your browser, you control its retention. When you delete it from localStorage, it is permanently removed.

Usage and Analytics Data

We retain anonymized usage and analytics data for up to 12 months to improve our Service. This data cannot be linked back to individual users.

Payment Records

Stripe retains payment transaction records according to their retention policy and applicable legal requirements, typically for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Resume SuperHero is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information and terminate the child's account.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account or provided information to our Service, please contact us immediately at partners@theaisuperheroes.com.

These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your home country. By using Resume SuperHero, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may include countries that do not have the same data protection laws as your home country. We will take measures to ensure that such transfers comply with applicable privacy laws.
