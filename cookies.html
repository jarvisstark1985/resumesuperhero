Cookie Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status. Cookies can be either temporary (session cookies) or persistent (stored for longer periods).

Resume SuperHero uses cookies and local storage technologies to enhance your user experience and provide core functionality. This policy explains how we use these technologies and how you can manage them.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

Resume SuperHero primarily relies on local storage rather than traditional cookies. This approach prioritizes your privacy by keeping your data in your browser, on your device, under your control.

Essential Functionality

We use storage technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and ensure the platform functions correctly.

User Experience Enhancement

Local storage helps us remember your dashboard settings, theme preferences, and personalization choices so you have a consistent experience each time you visit.

Resume and Data Management

Your resume content, application history, and coaching data are stored locally on your device, giving you complete control over your sensitive information.

Types of Data We Store

Resume SuperHero stores the following information in your browser's local storage under the key rsh_data:

Local Storage Contents (rsh_data)

  • • User Preferences: Theme settings, language selection, and interface customization choices
  • • Resume Data: Your resume content, sections, and formatting preferences
  • • Dashboard State: Your navigation state, expanded/collapsed sections, and view preferences
  • • Session Information: Your authentication token and user session data
  • • Application History: Records of your job applications and coaching interactions

Important: All data stored in local storage is kept exclusively in your browser on your device. This data is not automatically sent to our servers and remains under your complete control. You can clear this data at any time using your browser's storage management tools.

Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

Chrome

Click the menu icon (three dots) → Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data → Manage all cookies and site data

Firefox

Click the menu icon (three lines) → Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Manage Data

Safari

Safari menu → Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Click the menu icon (three dots) → Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data

Note: Disabling cookies may affect some features of Resume SuperHero. However, since we primarily use local storage, you'll retain most functionality.

Local Storage Management

Since Resume SuperHero relies heavily on local storage, you can directly manage this data through your browser's developer tools:

Clear Local Storage (All Browsers)

  1. Open Developer Tools (Press F12 or right-click → Inspect)
  2. Navigate to the "Application" or "Storage" tab
  3. Select "Local Storage" from the left sidebar
  4. Find "resumesuperhero.com" and click to select it
  5. Look for the key "rsh_data" and delete it, or right-click and select "Delete"

Clear All Site Data

You can also clear all data for Resume SuperHero (including local storage and cookies) using your browser's "Clear Site Data" or "Clear Browsing Data" feature. Search your browser's settings for "Clear data" or "Clear cache."

Warning: Clearing local storage will delete your locally stored resume data, preferences, and dashboard state. We recommend exporting your resume before clearing storage.
